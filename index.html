FAQ

Is it really free?

Yes. It is free to join and check availability. You can tip at the end based on your experience.

Do I need to pay in advance?

No. There is no payment today. Free booking means you can tip at the end based on your experience.

How long is the tour?

Usually around 2 to 2.5 hours, depending on group pace and questions.

What language is the tour in?

The tour is in English.

Is the meeting point central?

Yes. The meeting point is in central Porto. We'll confirm availability and send the meeting point by WhatsApp.

Can I cancel?

Yes. Plans change when traveling. Just send us a WhatsApp message as early as you can.

What happens if it rains?

Porto weather can change quickly. We usually walk in light rain, and we will confirm any important updates by WhatsApp.

Do I need cash for the tip?

Cash is useful and simple for tips. If you do not have cash, ask the guide what options are available.

Can families join?

Yes. The tour is relaxed and friendly for couples, families, friends, and first-time visitors.
