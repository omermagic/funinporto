Is it really free?

Yes. It is free to join and reserve. At the end, you decide how much to tip based on your experience.

How long is the tour?

Usually around 2 to 2.5 hours, depending on group pace and questions.

Where is the meeting point?

A central Porto meeting point is sent after booking by WhatsApp.

Can families join?

Yes. The tour is relaxed and friendly for couples, families, friends, and first-time visitors.
